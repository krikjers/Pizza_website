FAQ

Which type of pizza should I make?

This depends on your personal style. If you like fluffy and crunchy pizza, go for the Neapolitan style pizza. If you prefer larger slices with a crispy crust, go for the New York style pizza.


How long does it take to make a pizza?

The dough fermentation takes the longest time - Up to a few days. However, shaping the pizza, adding toppings and cooking is usually completed within 30 minutes.
